About Knowledge Institute of Science & Technology

Growing institution dedicated to quality education in Health and Management Sciences

Who We Are

Knowledge Institute of Science & Technology (KIST) is a growing institution dedicated to delivering quality education in Health and Management Sciences.

Located in Juba, South Sudan, KIST provides accessible, practical education that prepares students for meaningful careers and contributes to national development.

Our Mission

To provide transformative education that builds professional skills and contributes to national development.

Accreditation Status

KIST is in the final stage of securing full accreditation from the Ministry of Higher Education, Science & Technology – South Sudan.
