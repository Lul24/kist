# Knowledge Institute of Science & Technology (KIST) Website

[![Netlify Status](https://api.netlify.com/api/v1/badges/your-badge-id/deploy-status)](https://kist-edu.netlify.app)

Official website for Knowledge Institute of Science & Technology - a premier educational institution in Juba, South Sudan.

## 🌐 Live Website

Visit the live site: **[kist-edu.netlify.app](https://kist-edu.netlify.app)**

## 📋 About the Project

KIST is a growing institution dedicated to delivering quality education in Health Sciences, Management Sciences, and Education. This website serves as a digital presence to showcase academic programs, facilitate admissions, and provide information to prospective students.

## ✨ Features

- 🏠 **Home Page** - Hero slider, partner affiliations, campus life gallery
- 📖 **About Page** - Who we are, mission, accreditation status, campus locations
- 🎓 **Academics Page** - Program structure, learning modes, faculty overview
- 📚 **Courses Page** - Detailed course listings with tuition fees
- 📝 **Admissions Page** - Entry requirements, application process, tuition fees
- 👨‍🏫 **Faculty Page** - Leadership team and department faculty profiles
- 📞 **Contact Page** - Contact form with Netlify integration, map, FAQ
- ❤️ **Donation Feature** - Email-based donation inquiries
